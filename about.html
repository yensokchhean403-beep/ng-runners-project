Who We Are

NextGen Runners Club was founded with a vision to build more than just a running group. We wanted to create a mindful community where physical fitness meets mental clarity and personal growth.

Whether you are a beginner taking your first steps or an experienced marathoner, our weekly running sessions combined with mindfulness practices provide the perfect environment to challenge your body and calm your mind.
